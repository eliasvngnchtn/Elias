    FAMILIAR FACES

    To me personally, the way we are is rather boring. I paint faces, looking for more understanding to the complicated beings that we are.  Curiously seeking for perspective.

    By painting myself and neglecting the existing colours that we recognize I create my own reality by giving myself more meaning.
